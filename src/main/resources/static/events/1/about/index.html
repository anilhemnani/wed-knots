Welcome to Bekal, Kerala!

We are delighted to invite you to celebrate the wedding of Pratibha and Karthik in the scenic town of Bekal!
Pratibha, born in Bhavnagar (Gujarat), and Karthik, born in Nileshwar (Kerala), both grew up in London and are coming together for a unique celebration.
Their wedding will beautifully blend North and South Indian traditions, promising a joyful and memorable experience for everyone.
To help you plan your journey, we have listed the best train and flight options below. We recommend arriving in the morning at the suggested airport or station, so you have plenty of time to relax and enjoy the festivities—especially if you are traveling from afar!

Many guests may wish to explore Kerala while visiting for the wedding. The flight and train options listed below are suggested only to help those who are coming directly for the wedding. If you plan to travel around Kerala, feel free to choose routes and timings that best suit your itinerary!

Recommended Travel Options

  • By Flight: Nearest airport is Mangalore International Airport (IXE), about 65 km from Bekal. Check flights on Skyscanner
  • By Train: Nearest railway station is Kasaragod, about 12 km from Bekal. Kanhangad station is also nearby.
