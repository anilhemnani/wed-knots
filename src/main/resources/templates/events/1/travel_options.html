Welcome to Bekal!

Bekal is a beautiful spot in the north of Kerala. We’re so excited to welcome you! Below are some travel suggestions to help you plan your trip.

Many guests love to explore Kerala while visiting for the wedding. The flight and train options below are just suggestions for those coming directly for the wedding. If you’d like to travel around Kerala, choose the routes that work best for you! We recommend arriving in the morning at the suggested airport or station, so you have plenty of time to recover, relax and enjoy the festivities—especially if you are traveling from afar!
