What is WedKnots?

WedKnots – The Smart Wedding Concierge is a comprehensive platform designed to simplify the complex process of planning and executing Indian weddings. Whether you're hosting a wedding or attending one, our platform brings everything together in one place.

With our intuitive interface and powerful features, event hosts can effortlessly manage guest lists, track RSVPs, coordinate travel arrangements, and stay connected with guests through integrated WhatsApp communication.

Guests enjoy a seamless experience - from receiving invitations to managing their attendance, adding travel details, and communicating with event hosts.

Manage your wedding events with confidence and elegance
