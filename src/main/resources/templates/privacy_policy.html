2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Event Management: To organize and coordinate wedding events, manage guest lists, and track RSVPs
  • Communication: To send invitations, updates, and event-related notifications via WhatsApp or other channels
  • Travel Coordination: To assist with travel arrangements, pickups, and accommodations
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access and maintain data security
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 With Event Hosts

Your RSVP status, attendee information, and travel details are shared with the wedding event hosts who invited you.

4.2 With Other Guests

We do not share your personal information with other guests unless you explicitly consent or it's necessary for event coordination (e.g., shared transportation).

4.3 Third-Party Services

We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in:

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

To exercise these rights, please contact the event administrator or system support.
